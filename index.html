In 2012 , I lost my best friend Alexander Eu to cancer.

￼

I thought we could grow old together and talk about fashion forever. I felt lost, angry, useless unable to help and was terribly depressed after he is gone.

￼

To help me get myself up and out of this dark depression, I seek help from friends and also from a charity group called Shine Cancer Support.

￼

Shine is the only UK charity that exists exclusively to support adults in 20s, 30s and 40s who have experience a cancer diagnosis.

Shine vision is that every adult in the UK living with cancer can access the help and support that they need in a way that suit their lifestyle and that they are part of a confident, supportive and empowered community of young adults living with cancer.

Their mission is to provide tailored information and peer support for anyone in their 20s, 30s and 40s diagnosed with any cancer. Shine helped me to regain my positive outlook of my own life and return to work.
